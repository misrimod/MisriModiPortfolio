Designing for Ethical and Humane Warehouses

How we solved chaos in a bustling e-commerce warehouse, one design step at a time.

The Challenge

  • Problem: Fragmented communication and fluctuating workflows disrupt efficiency.
  • Key Players: Pickers, Packers, and Shippers.
  • Peak Hours: 10 AM - 1 PM and 8 PM - 9 PM, with 50% order surges.

We needed a flexible, real-time system that integrates their tasks without rigid workflows.

Our Solution

Pickers (Mobile View)

  • Optimized task list and routes.
  • Real-time updates for priority changes.
  • Error reporting and progress tracking.

Packers (Dashboard)

  • Incoming item alerts and status tracking.
  • Surge management with overflow areas.

Shippers (Dashboard)

  • Label printing queue with quick actions.
  • Final checks for order accuracy.

Our Design Journey

  1. Step 1: Identified key issues through role-based analysis.
  2. Step 2: Brainstormed solutions tailored to each role.
  3. Step 3: Developed low-fidelity prototypes for mobile and dashboard interfaces.
  4. Step 4: Iterated designs based on team feedback.

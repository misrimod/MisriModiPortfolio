Project 1: Designing for Ethical and Humane Warehouses

Recently participated in a designathon with my professional fraternity, Alpha Theta Delta. The prompt was to improve efficiency in an e-commerce warehouse. Our team initially struggled to balance the needs of diverse roles, such as pickers, managers, and inventory trackers, without compromising usability. I proposed dividing the problem into sections based on roles and brainstorming tailored solutions for each. After many discussions to ensure every perspective was considered, we created a system with distinct interfaces and designs for each role, optimizing workflows while maintaining a cohesive design language. This experience emphasized the importance of collaboration and flexibility in problem-solving.
